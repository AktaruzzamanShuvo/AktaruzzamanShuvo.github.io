Bio

I am an Electrical and Electronic Engineer with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology, completed in November 2023. During my undergraduate studies, I specialized in power systems, gaining in-depth knowledge of electrical networks, renewable energy integration, and sustainable power solutions.

Most recently, I served as a Lecturer at BGMEA University of Fashion & Technology (BUFT), where I taught and mentored students in engineering disciplines, fostering both academic and practical understanding. My academic journey began at Gaibandha Boys' High School (SSC, 2016) and continued at Gaibandha Government College (HSC, 2018).

I'm passionate about energy systems and research, and I hope to make a difference in sustainable power by learning, innovating, and sharing knowledge with others.

Research Interests

My research focuses on advancing sustainable energy solutions through the following areas:

  • Hybrid Renewable Energy Systems: Optimizing solar, wind, and other renewables for better efficiency and grid stability.

  • Electric Vehicles: Developing control strategies for efficient power use, battery management, and integration into smart grids.

  • Microgrid & Power System Protection: Enhancing microgrid stability, fault detection, and adaptive control for grid resilience.

  • Solar Energy: Improving photovoltaic systems, storage solutions, and integrating solar power into smart grids.
