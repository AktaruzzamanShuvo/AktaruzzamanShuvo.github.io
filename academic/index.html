An Overview of My Academic Journey

UNDERGRADUATE

    I had the privilege of completing my undergraduate studies with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST) on 26th November 2023. My academic journey commenced in Spring 2019, during which I completed 165 credit hours, covering a comprehensive range of foundational and advanced topics in Electrical and Electronic Engineering.

    Throughout the program, I built a robust foundation in both theoretical knowledge and practical application.

    Some of the core courses I completed include:

    • Electric Circuit Analysis I & II
    • Electronics I & II
    • Energy Conversion I, II & III
    • Electromagnetics
    • Solid State Devices
    • Signals and Linear Systems
    • Digital Electronics I (DLD)
    • Digital Signal Processing I
    • Power Systems I & II
    • Power Electronics
    • Power System Protection
    • Microprocessor, Interfacing and System Design
    • Computer Architecture
    • Optical Fiber Communication
    • VLSI I
    • Control Systems

    Each course was accompanied by laboratory sessions that enabled me to apply concepts through simulations and hands-on experimentation.

    I gained proficiency in a wide range of industry-standard software tools, including:

    OrCAD:PSPICE, Proteus,Quartus,MATLAB,Simulink,AutoCAD,PowerWorld Simulator,Cadence:Virtuoso,HOMER Pro,Origin,among others.

    Alongside software skills, I also acquired practical experience with hardware platforms and embedded systems such as:

    Arduino Uno,Arduino Nano,ESP8266 NodeMCU,MPU 8086,FPGA board,and various integrated sensor systems.

    As part of my undergraduate studies, I conducted a thesis in the field of nanomaterials,under the guidance of the esteemed Professor Dr. Meganur Rahman. The title of my thesis was: "Investigation of Electric Properties of Bismuth Ferrite Nanoparticles at Various Temperatures", for which I was honored with the highest academic distinction (Grade: 4.00/4.00).

    My academic performance consistently placed me among the top 5% of my batch, earning me tuition fee waivers in many semesters as recognition of academic excellence.

    Beyond the classroom, I actively participated in seminars, technical competitions, sports events, and cultural programs. These experiences enhanced my communication, leadership, adaptability, and ability to collaborate effectively within diverse teams.

Higher Secondary Certificate

    I completed my Higher Secondary Certificate (HSC) from Gaibandha Government College in 2018, under the Science group. I enrolled in the college in 2016, and my academic session officially spanned 2016–2017, culminating in the HSC examination in 2018.

    Those two years were some of the most intense yet formative periods of my academic life. College life was undoubtedly a roller-coaster of emotions—demanding, disciplined, and filled with relentless academic pressure. The routine was rigorous, with back-to-back lecture classes, regular tests, and a constant stream of study responsibilities. It often felt overwhelming.

    Yet, amidst all the hustle, there were moments that I truly cherished. I still remember Class Day, a time of celebration and camaraderie, which offered a welcome break from the academic load and created lasting memories with friends and mentors. That period, though hectic and challenging, played a pivotal role in shaping my resilience, work ethic, and focus.

    Despite the demands, I remained committed to my studies and successfully passed the HSC examination in 2018. Soon after, my focus shifted toward preparing for university admission—a new chapter filled with hope, ambition, and determination to pursue a career in engineering.

    Looking back, those college days at Gaibandha Government College were not just about exams and lectures—they were about growth, perseverance, and the friendships that carried us through.
