An Overview of My Academic Journey

UNDERGRADUATE

    I had the privilege of completing my undergraduate studies with a Bachelor of Science in Electrical and Electronic Engineering (B.Sc. in EEE) from Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology (AUST) on 26th November 2023. My academic journey commenced in Spring 2019, during which I completed 165 credit hours, covering a comprehensive range of foundational and advanced topics in Electrical and Electronic Engineering.

    Throughout the program, I built a robust foundation in both theoretical knowledge and practical application.

    Some of the core courses I completed include:

    • Electric Circuit Analysis I & II
    • Electronics I & II
    • Energy Conversion I, II & III
    • Electromagnetics
    • Solid State Devices
    • Signals and Linear Systems
    • Digital Electronics I (DLD)
    • Digital Signal Processing I
    • Power Systems I & II
    • Power Electronics
    • Power System Protection
    • Microprocessor, Interfacing and System Design
    • Computer Architecture
    • Optical Fiber Communication
    • VLSI I
    • Control Systems

    Each course was accompanied by laboratory sessions that enabled me to apply concepts through simulations and hands-on experimentation.I gained proficiency in a wide range of industry-standard software tools, including: OrCAD:PSPICE, Proteus,Quartus , MATLAB,Simulink, AutoCAD,PowerWorld Simulator, Cadence:Virtuoso, HOMER Pro, Origin, among others.Alongside software skills, I also acquired practical experience with hardware platforms and embedded systems such as: Arduino Uno,Arduino Nano, ESP8266 NodeMCU,MPU 8086,FPGA boards,and various integrated sensor systems.

    As part of my undergraduate studies, I conducted a thesis in the field of nanomaterials,under the guidance of the esteemed Professor Dr. Meganur Rahman. The title of my thesis was: "Investigation of Electric Properties of Bismuth Ferrite Nanoparticles at Various Temperatures", for which I was honored with the highest academic distinction (Grade: 4.00/4.00).

    My academic performance consistently placed me among the top 5% of my batch, earning me tuition fee waivers in many semesters as recognition of academic excellence.

    Beyond the classroom, I actively participated in seminars, technical competitions, sports events, and cultural programs. These experiences enhanced my communication, leadership, adaptability, and ability to collaborate effectively within diverse teams.

    This section also features cherished memories from my years at AUST, reflecting a vibrant and intellectually fulfilling undergraduate journey that laid the foundation for my future academic and professional pursuits.
